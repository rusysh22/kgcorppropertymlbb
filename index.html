Tournament Rules

  • Best of 3 (BO3) format will be used for all matches: A team needs to win 2 out of 3 matches to win the series
      - Example scenarios BO3:
      • Team A wins first 2 matches → Series ends 2-0
      • Team A wins first match, Team B wins second match, Team A wins third match → Series ends 2-1
  • Winning team receives 3 points, losing team receives 0 points
  • 15-minute grace period is allowed after the scheduled time; teams arriving after this period will forfeit the match (WO)
  • Top 2 teams (Champion and Runner-up) will represent KG CORPORATE/PROPERTY in the next level
